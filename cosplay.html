Cosplay

My favorite hobby to indulge in is cosplay! Cosplay is the act of dressing up and wearing things to make yourself look like a character; Whether it be from a video game (my favorite), movie, tv show, or even a personal character, you can dress up as anything. For my costumes, I usually tinker certain pieces or choose styles, but most of the work is done by my friend Courtney Kerst. The pieces I make are the weapons/extras! This can be seen in the picture captioned "Ahri" below. I made the light blue tail pictured to my right using a cellophane material around a wire base I constructed myself. The cellophane was applied with hot glue to keep it on the wire, but a heat gun to make the sides seamless.

All of my cosplay work can be found on @qlemons.cosplay on Instagram if you would like to see more/keep up to date on my projects.
